Donating well

Tips for Donors & Shoppers

Good donations keep reuse centers running. Here's how to make the most of giving and getting.

🎁 Before You Donate

  • Clean items before dropping them off — dirty donations are often discarded
  • Test electronics and small appliances to confirm they work
  • Call ahead for large items like furniture or appliances to confirm space
  • Remove personal information from electronics (do a factory reset)
  • Include all parts, remotes, and accessories when possible
  • Donate clothing in season — summer clothes donated in winter may not be used

🛒 Smart Reuse Shopping

  • Visit early — freshly donated items move fast at popular stores
  • Check return policies before purchasing electronics or appliances
  • Bring measurements when shopping for furniture or building materials
  • Ask about discount days — many stores offer rotating sales
  • Reuse stores are excellent sources for craft, art, and DIY supplies
  • Sign up for store newsletters to hear about large donation arrivals
