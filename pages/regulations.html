Plans & documents

Plans & Publications

Key planning documents, guides, and reference materials from the County of Hawai'i Department of Environmental Management.

📄
Zero Waste Implementation Plan — The County's roadmap for achieving zero waste goals, adopted in March 2009. Includes strategies for reduction, recycling, composting, and community education.
📄
Solid Waste Management Plan — Long-range planning document outlining the County's solid waste infrastructure, capacity, and program development. Required by State law.
📄
Plastic Bag Reduction Ordinance FAQ — Answers to common questions for businesses and consumers about the plastic bag ban, exemptions, and enforcement.
📄
Map & Directions to County Solid Waste Facilities — Printable guide to all 21 County Recycling and Transfer Stations, including addresses, hours, and accepted materials.
📄
Request for Waiver of Tipping Fees — Form for nonprofit organizations and government agencies seeking waiver of disposal fees related to illegal dump remediation.
📄
GPS Locations for Recycling Facilities (ArcGIS) — Machine-readable coordinate data for all County recycling and waste disposal facilities, updated annually.

Educational materials

Recycling Publications

Guides, brochures, and fact sheets to help residents and businesses recycle correctly and reduce waste at the source.

📘
What Goes Where Guide

A quick-reference guide for sorting recyclables, greenwaste, hazardous materials, and general trash at home and at the transfer station.

🌱
Backyard Composting Guide

Published in partnership with UH CTAHR. Step-by-step instructions for home composting of food waste and yard trimmings.

🏫
School Recycling Toolkit

Curriculum materials and setup guides for K–12 schools implementing recycling and composting programs. Available through the DEM education program.

🏢
Business Recycling Guide

Information for commercial establishments on setting up recycling programs, licensing requirements, and licensed hauler contacts.
