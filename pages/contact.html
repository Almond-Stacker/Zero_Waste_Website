Contact by Topic

Not sure who to call? Use this guide to find the right contact for your specific question.

♻️
Recycling Questions

Call the Recycling Information Line at (808) 961-8942 for help identifying where to bring specific materials.

☣️
Hazardous Waste

Call the HHW Infoline at (808) 961-8554 for event schedules and material questions, or contact Greg Perry, EnviroServices, at (808) 330-1604.

🚯
Illegal Dumping

To report illegal dumping, call (808) 961-4354 or use the online report form.

🏫
School Education

To request a school presentation or educational materials, contact DEM at (808) 961-8270. Learn more →

📋
Ordinance & Compliance

Questions about the plastic bag or polystyrene ordinances? Email bring-ur-bag@hawaiicounty.gov or call the main DEM line.

📰
Media Inquiries

Media contacts and press release requests go to the main DEM office at (808) 961-8270 or swd@hawaiicounty.gov.
