HI-5 Redemption

The Hawai‘i Deposit Beverage Container Program (HI-5) is a State of Hawai‘i funded program that places a 5¢ redeemable deposit on each qualified beverage container. Instead of just tossing your recyclables, why not save them and bring them to one of the 15 convenient redemption centers located island wide? Just look for the HI 5¢ label on your glass, plastic and metal containers, and take them to any redemption center to get cash back and help protect the environment at the same time.
