What you can find

Facility Types on the Map

The Mālama Map covers all major County waste management and recycling facilities across Hawai'i Island.

♻️
Recycling & Transfer Stations

21 stations island-wide accepting recyclables, greenwaste, bulky items, and general trash. Open 8 a.m. – 4 p.m. daily (closed major holidays).

☣️
HHW Collection Events

Rotating hazardous waste collection events in Hilo, Kona, Waimea, and Ka'ū. Check the map for the next event near you.

🪴
Free Mulch Pickup

Free wood chip mulch is available at the West and East Hawai'i Organics Facilities. The map shows current availability and directions.

🔄
Reuse Centers

Hilo and Kona Reuse Centers accept donated goods and offer free items to the public. Find hours, addresses, and accepted materials on the map.

🏭
Organics Facilities

West and East Hawai'i Organics Facilities accept residential greenwaste and provide free mulch. The map shows hours and directions.

🏛️
Main Landfill & Reload Facilities

West Hawai'i Sanitary Landfill and East Hawai'i Reload Facility locations are included for reference and permitted hauler access.

Tips

How to Use the Map

1
Search by address to find the closest facilities to your home or business. The map will show distance and driving directions.
2
Filter by material type — select what you need to dispose of (e.g., motor oil, electronics, greenwaste) and the map will highlight facilities that accept it.
3
Click any facility pin to see hours of operation, accepted materials, contact information, and directions via Google Maps.
4
Check for HHW events by selecting the "Hazardous Waste Events" layer — event dates and times will be shown on each pin's popup.
📞
Can't Find What You're Looking For?

Call the DEM Recycling Information Line at (808) 961-8942 for help finding the right disposal location for any material. We're here to help you keep Hawai'i Island clean.
