DEM Announces 2026 HHW Collection Schedule

The Department of Environmental Management has released the 2026 schedule for Household Hazardous Waste collection events across Hawai'i Island, including new Waimea-area dates. Events run from April through December at rotating locations in Hilo, Kona, and Ka'ū.

Kealakehe Transfer Station Temporary Closure — February 20–21

The Kealakehe Recycling and Transfer Station will be temporarily closed February 20–21, 2026 for scheduled maintenance. Residents are encouraged to use the Kona or Kawaihae stations during this period.

Zero Waste Program Marks 17th Anniversary — County Reaffirms 2035 Goal

The County of Hawai'i Department of Environmental Management commemorated the 17th anniversary of the Zero Waste Mission, adopted by the County Council in January 2009. DEM Director reaffirmed the County's commitment to the 2035 zero waste goal.

New Composting Pilot Program Launches in Hilo

DEM launched a residential food waste collection pilot in select Hilo neighborhoods, diverting organic waste from the landfill to the East Hawai'i Organics Facility. Program expansion planned for 2026 pending evaluation results.

November 4, 2025 — Greenwaste & composting →

Aug
County Receives State Grant for Electronics Recycling Expansion

The County of Hawai'i was awarded a $180,000 State grant to expand electronics recycling access to rural and underserved communities across Hawai'i Island, including new drop-off events in Ka'ū and Kohala.

August 20, 2025 — Electronics recycling →

May
Polystyrene Ordinance Enforcement — Reminder to Food Vendors

DEM issued a reminder to all County food vendors that the Polystyrene Foam Ordinance remains in full effect. Inspections and fines will increase through summer 2025. Vendors needing guidance on compliant containers are encouraged to contact DEM.

May 12, 2025 — Polystyrene ordinance →

Jan
2025 Transfer Station Hours Updated — New Year Holiday Schedule

All County Recycling and Transfer Stations were closed January 1, 2025 for New Year's Day. Normal operating hours (8:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.) resumed January 2. The 2025 holiday closure schedule is available from DEM.
