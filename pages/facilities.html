Solid Waste Facilities

COUNTY OF HAWAI'I ENVIRONMENTAL
MANAGEMENT

The Solid Waste Division manages 21 Recycling & Transfer Stations, 2 Greenwaste Facilities, and additional reuse and disposal services throughout Hawai'i Island to support our zero waste mission.

The Solid Waste Division is responsible for all recycling and reuse programs at the County of Hawai'i Recycling and Transfer Stations, and the East and West Hawai'i Organics "Greenwaste" Facilities. This includes drop-off recycling, HI-5 redemption, Reuse Centers, and mulch production services.

Greenwaste & Organics Facilities

Drop off yard waste and food discards at either of our two island-wide organics facilities.

East Hawai'i Organics Facility

  • Location: Hilo, HI
  • Hours: Daily 7:00 AM–4:15 PM (7 days a week)
  • Services: Greenwaste drop-off, mulch production

West Hawai'i Organics Facility

  • Location: Waikoloa, HI
  • Hours: Mon–Sat 7:00 AM–4:15 PM
  • Services: Greenwaste drop-off, mulch production

Reuse Centers

Reuse Centers offer free gently used household items to the public. 8 locations are co-located with Recycling & Transfer Stations island-wide.

Hilo Reuse Center

  • Location: Ho'olaulima Rd. (before Transfer Station, makai side), Hilo
  • Hours: Daily 8:00 AM–4:00 PM
  • Closed: New Year's Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day

Kealakehe Reuse Center

  • Location: 74-625 Hale Māka'i Place, Kailua-Kona
  • Hours: Daily 8:00 AM–4:00 PM
  • Closed: New Year's Day, Ironman World Championship, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day

Additional Reuse Centers

  • 6 additional Reuse Centers are available at Transfer Stations island-wide
  • Contact (808) 961-8270 for locations and hours

Landfill & Reload Facility

The Solid Waste Division operates one active sanitary landfill and one reload facility for Hawai'i Island.

West Hawai'i Sanitary Landfill

  • Location: West Hawai'i
  • Services: Disposal of non-recyclable solid waste
  • Contact: (808) 961-8270

East Hawai'i Reload Facility

  • Location: East Hawai'i (Hilo area)
  • Services: Transfer of collected waste for transport to landfill
  • Contact: (808) 961-8270
